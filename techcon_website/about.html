Our History

TechCon has been at the forefront of technological innovation and industry collaboration since its inception. Our journey represents decades of bringing together the brightest minds in technology.

15 Years Running
50K+ Attendees
500+ Speakers
100+ Countries
2010

The Beginning

TechCon was founded with 500 attendees in Silicon Valley, focusing on emerging web technologies and the early days of mobile computing.

2015

Global Expansion

We went international, hosting satellite events across Europe and Asia, reaching over 10,000 participants worldwide.

2020

Digital Transformation

Pivoted to virtual format during the pandemic, creating innovative online experiences that connected 25,000 attendees globally.

2024

The Future is Now

Our most ambitious event yet, featuring AI breakthroughs, quantum computing, and sustainable technology innovations.
